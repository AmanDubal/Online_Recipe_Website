Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Sugar syrup: 2 cups sugar, 1 cup water, cardamom
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda, add water to make a soft dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Dip in warm sugar syrup for 1-2 hours, serve hot or cold.
