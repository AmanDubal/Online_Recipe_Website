Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Saffron strands, cardamom
  • Sugar syrup: 2 cups sugar, 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, and baking powder, ferment for 12 hours.
  2. Pour batter into hot ghee in circular motions, fry until crisp.
  3. Dip in warm sugar syrup for 1-2 minutes, serve hot.
