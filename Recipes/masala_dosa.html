Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice batter (dosa batter)
  • 1 cup potato filling (masala)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Spices: Mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric, curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Spread dosa batter on a hot griddle, cook until golden and crispy.
  2. Fill with potato masala, fold, and serve hot with chutney.
