Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Spices: Garam masala, ginger, garlic

Instructions

  1. Boil lentils and kidney beans until soft, then mash slightly.
  2. Saut√© onions, ginger, and garlic until golden brown.
  3. Add mashed lentils, cream, and spices, simmer until flavors blend.
  4. Serve hot with rice or naan.
