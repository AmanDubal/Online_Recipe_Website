Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, baking soda
  • 1 cup milk, vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, beat until smooth.
  2. Pour into greased pans, bake at 350Â°F for 30-35 minutes.
  3. Cool, frost with chocolate ganache or buttercream.
  4. Serve slices with a scoop of ice cream.
