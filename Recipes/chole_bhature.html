Chole Bhature

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (chole)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Spices: Cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala

Instructions

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight, then boil until tender.
  2. Mix flour with water and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Roll out dough into small circles and deep-fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with chole, chopped onions, and tomatoes.
