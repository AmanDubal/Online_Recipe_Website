Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 500g spinach (palak)
  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Spices: Cumin seeds, turmeric, garam masala

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach, blend into a paste, and set aside.
  2. Saut√© onions and tomatoes, add spices and cook until soft.
  3. Add spinach paste, simmer, then stir in paneer cubes.
  4. Serve hot with naan or rice.
