Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 500g paneer cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Marinate paneer with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  2. Skewer paneer cubes and grill until golden brown.
  3. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
