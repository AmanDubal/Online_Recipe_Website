Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup boiled potatoes, peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, coriander powder
  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, chili powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour with water, knead into dough, and let it rest.
  2. Saut√© spices, add mashed potatoes and peas, cook until dry.
  3. Roll dough, fill with mixture, seal into triangles, and fry until golden.
  4. Serve hot with chutney.
