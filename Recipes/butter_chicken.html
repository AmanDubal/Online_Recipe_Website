Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Spices: Garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and yogurt for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken.
  3. Add tomato puree and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Pour in cream and let the dish cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.
