Cauliflower Manchurian

Ingredients

  • 1 medium cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce, vinegar
  • Spices: Garlic, ginger, green chilies, pepper
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cornflour, and water to make a batter.
  2. Dip cauliflower in batter, fry until golden brown, set aside.
  3. Saut√© garlic, ginger, green chilies, add soy sauce, vinegar, and water.
  4. Add fried cauliflower, toss until coated, garnish with spring onions.
  5. Serve hot as a snack or with fried rice.
