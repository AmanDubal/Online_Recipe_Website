Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Spices: Garam masala, chili powder, kasuri methi
  • Butter, ginger-garlic paste

Instructions

  1. Saut√© ginger-garlic paste in butter, add tomato puree, cook until oil separates.
  2. Add spices, cream, and paneer, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Garnish with kasuri methi, serve hot with naan or rice.
