Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Spices: Turmeric, chili powder, coriander seeds
  • Curry leaves, mustard seeds

Instructions

  1. Saut√© onions, add spices and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add coconut milk, bring to boil, add fish and simmer until cooked.
  3. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves, serve hot with rice.
