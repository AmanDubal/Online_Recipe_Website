Rogan Josh

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb/mutton
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Spices: Kashmiri red chili, fennel seeds, cardamom, cloves

Instructions

  1. Saut√© onions until golden brown, add spices and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Add meat and brown, then stir in yogurt and simmer until tender.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
