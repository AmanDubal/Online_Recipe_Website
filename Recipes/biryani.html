Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice
  • 1 kg chicken or vegetables
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices: Saffron, bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes, then boil until partially cooked.
  2. Marinate chicken/vegetables with spices and yogurt for 1 hour.
  3. Layer rice and chicken/vegetables in a pot, add saffron-infused milk.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat until fully done, garnish with fried onions.
  5. Serve hot with raita and salad.
